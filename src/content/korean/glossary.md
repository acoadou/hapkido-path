Terminology may vary by school, federation, language background, and romanization system.

- **dojang** — training hall.
- **dobok** — training uniform.
- **sabumnim** — instructor or teacher title.
- **geup** — color-belt grade.
- **dan** — black-belt degree.
- **hap** — harmony or coordination.
- **ki** — energy, spirit, or internal power depending on context.
- **do** — way or path.
- **uke** — receiving partner in these notes.
- **tori** — applying partner in these notes.
