## Short reminder

Start low, make a round path from shoulder to opposite hip, and stand only when balance is available.

## Step-by-step breakdown

1. Begin from kneeling or a low squat.
2. Place the lead hand and create a rounded arm frame.
3. Tuck the chin and look toward the belt knot or hip.
4. Roll from shoulder across the back to the opposite hip.
5. Finish seated, kneeling, or standing depending on control.

## Key details

- Keep the head off the mat.
- Exhale during the roll.
- Progress height gradually.

## Common mistakes

- Rolling over the neck.
- Slapping the mat too late.
- Standing before balance returns.
