## Short reminder

Use a calm straight-line entry to connect your hand, elbow, and center before adding any lock or takedown idea.

## Step-by-step breakdown

1. Begin from a stable stance and relaxed guard.
2. Extend through the center without reaching past your balance.
3. Keep the elbow heavy and connected to the ribs.
4. Reset slowly and repeat on both sides.

## Key details

- Do not chase the partner's hand.
- Keep the shoulders down.
- Let the stance support the arm.

## Common mistakes

- Locking the elbow rigidly.
- Raising the shoulder under pressure.
- Looking at the hands instead of the partner's posture.

## Safety notes

Use this as a structure drill before adding joint pressure.
